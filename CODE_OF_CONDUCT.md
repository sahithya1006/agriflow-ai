# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to a respectful, inclusive, and harassment-free project environment for contributors and users.

## Expected Behavior

- Use welcoming and considerate language.
- Respect different viewpoints and experience levels.
- Give and accept constructive feedback.
- Focus discussion on what is best for the project and its users.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, insults, threats, or discriminatory language.
- Publishing private information without permission.
- Sustained disruption of project discussions or collaboration.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove comments, issues, commits, or other contributions that violate this code of conduct. Serious or repeated violations may result in temporary or permanent removal from project spaces.

## Reporting

Report concerns privately to the maintainers listed in the repository or through the security contact in `SECURITY.md`.
